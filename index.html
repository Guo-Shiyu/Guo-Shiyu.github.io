学书不成，学剑不成，

学节义不成，学文章不成,

学仙、学佛、学农、学圃,

俱不成。

And his soul is seasoned, his soul is very professional.

Only his body remains forever an amateur.

It tries and it misses, gets muddled, doesn't learn a thing.

Drunk and blind in its pleasures and its pains.



[译] Lua : 能够穿过“针孔”的语言
